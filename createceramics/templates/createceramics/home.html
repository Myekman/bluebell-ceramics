What we offer:

Pottery

If you want to get your hands dirty and create your own shapes of ceramic. For example, statues, mugs, candlesticks, plates and bowls. Book a session of turn. You get guidance and help from our talented turners. A booked appointment is a maximum of 4 hours long. We burn your beautiful creations, which are ready to pick up after 1 week. If you wish to paint your own pottery when it's ready, you are most welcome to book an appointment to paint it.
